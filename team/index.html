Sultan Sukiev, Director of Photography

Born in Moscow. During his study in VGIK Sultan has started his work as cameraman and director of photography in multiple cinema projects.

Filmography:

  • Monkey's Dream (feature film,2016)
  • Lost. The Last Hope (series, 2014)
  • Castle (Russian version, series, 2016-2017)
  • The Talent (short, 2017, Cannes Festival (Short Film Corner, Diversity in Cannes Official Selection)
  • Dowry (short, 2013)
  • Chorine (short, 2012)
  • Characters (short, 2012)

There are multiple Shorts, Advertisements, and Musical videos including videos in cooperation with Noise MS in Sultan's portfolio as well.

Sultan has his special vision which makes his every shot very meaningful and expressive.

Sergey Petrov, First Assistant Director

Outstanding professional with 5-years working experience in filmmaking sphere. That is what he is telling about himself:

“If you are effective First Assistant Director you should know almost everything about filmmaking, this is necessary for effective control during shooting process. I'm proud that I have experience in different fields of filmmaking, including creative roles and technical assistance. I have been a First Assistant Director for Russia TV, Match TV, in some feature films and in multiple advertisements and shorts. In addition to my First Assistant Director experience I'm a Producer for one of the Film Production companies in Moscow”.

Sergey keeps moving on and already has one short and one documentary as director.

Short films:

Alternative sound design:
